HOSUR SPORTS ACADEMY

SWIMMING

Swimming engages various muscle groups, including the core, arms, legs, and back. It helps tone and strengthen muscles, leading to improved overall muscle endurance and flexibility. It can help with weight loss or maintaining a healthy weight due to the energy expended during the activity.

TENNIS

Tennis is an excellent aerobic exercise that gets your heart pumping and improves cardiovascular fitness. The constant movement and short bursts of intense activity contribute to heart health.Tennis requires good hand-eye coordination and quick reflexes

ATHLETICS

Athletics encompasses a variety of competitive sports, including running, jumping, and throwing events. Participation in athletics promotes cardiovascular health, muscular strength, and flexibility. Athletes engage in disciplined training, fostering skills like teamwork and leadership.

HOCKEY

Hockey offers a dynamic and fast-paced workout that enhances cardiovascular fitness, endurance, and agility. The sport promotes muscular strength and coordination through activities such as skating, passing, and shooting.
